Case 2 - Someone's lying...

Scene Report

Four suspects claim where they were between 5:00 and 5:40.

Alex: "I arrived at 5:00 and left at 5:30."

Bella: "I was there from 5:10 to 5:40."

Charlie: "I came before Bella and left before 5:20."

Dani: "I didn’t overlap with Charlie at all."

Only one suspect is lying. Who is it?
