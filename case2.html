Case 2 - The Sneak

Briefing

Someone visited your house last night to hide a secret message. Five suspects were interviewed. Four have solid alibis. One does not. Identify who the visitor was and they will tell you where to look.

Statements:

Sox: "I was at the cinema from 19:00 to 21:30 with Mittens. He can confirm."

Francois: "I was home alone, but I was on a video call with my sister from 20:00 to 21:00."

Snuggles: "I was out walking my dog from 20:00 to 20:45, and I passed AH! at the coffee shop at 20:30."

Mittens: "I was reading at the library from 19:30 until closing at 21:00. You can ask the librarian."

AH!: "I stopped by the coffee shop at 20:15 and stayed until 20:45. Didn't see Snuggles, though."

Notes

  • The coffee shop has a record of AH! arriving at 20:15 and leaving at 20:45.
  • The librarian confirmed Mittens checked out a book at 21:00.
  • Video call logs show Francois was online from 20:00 to 21:00.
  • Movie tickets confirm Sox and Mittens entered the cinema at 19:05 and left at 21:35.
  • Snuggles claims to have seen AH! at 20:30, but AH! says they never saw him.
